COMPANY OVERVIEW

TechNova Solutions is a technology consulting and software development company specializing in web applications, cloud solutions, artificial intelligence, and enterprise software systems. The company serves clients across healthcare, education, finance, and retail industries. Its mission is to provide innovative and reliable technology solutions that improve business efficiency and customer experiences.

The company operates through multiple departments including Human Resources, Software Development, Quality Assurance, Project Management, Finance, Sales, Customer Support, and Information Technology.

EMPLOYEE LEAVE POLICY

The company provides several categories of leave to support employee well-being and work-life balance.

Annual Leave

Full-time employees are entitled to 24 days of paid annual leave per calendar year. Annual leave requests should be submitted at least seven days in advance through the employee management portal. Approval depends on project schedules and managerial consent.

Sick Leave

Employees receive 12 days of paid sick leave annually. A medical certificate may be required for absences exceeding two consecutive days.

Casual Leave

Employees may take up to 8 days of casual leave annually for personal matters and emergencies.

Maternity Leave

Eligible employees are entitled to 26 weeks of paid maternity leave in accordance with company policy and applicable labor regulations.

Paternity Leave

Eligible employees may avail 10 working days of paid paternity leave following the birth or adoption of a child.

Unpaid Leave

Employees who exhaust their paid leave balance may request unpaid leave, subject to managerial and HR approval.

Leave Approval Process
Employee submits leave request.
Reporting manager reviews request.
HR verifies leave balance.
Approval or rejection notification is sent.
Leave records are updated automatically.
Leave Carry Forward

A maximum of 10 unused annual leave days may be carried forward to the next calendar year.

EMPLOYEE ONBOARDING PROCESS

The onboarding process ensures smooth integration of new employees into the organization.

Pre-Joining Activities

After accepting an offer letter, candidates receive onboarding instructions and documentation requirements. They must submit identity proofs, educational certificates, address verification documents, bank account information, and tax-related documents.

Day One Orientation

New employees attend an orientation session covering:

Company history and culture
Organizational structure
Workplace policies
Employee code of conduct
Information security guidelines
Benefits and compensation overview
System Access Setup

The IT department provides:

Official email account
Employee ID
Laptop or workstation
VPN access
Collaboration tools access
Project management system credentials
Department Introduction

Employees are introduced to:

Team members
Reporting managers
Project leads
Internal communication channels
Training and Development

Mandatory training includes:

Workplace ethics
Cybersecurity awareness
Company procedures
Product knowledge
Role-specific technical training
Probation Period

New employees undergo a six-month probation period during which performance, attendance, communication, and technical skills are evaluated.

Onboarding Completion

Onboarding is considered complete after:

Mandatory training completion
Documentation verification
Successful probation review
Departmental approval
